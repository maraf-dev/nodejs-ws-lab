Question 4: What are the different values for the readyState a WebSocket can be in? Briefly describe what each state means. (Hint: check out the Mozilla WebSocket API)
CONNECTING (0): The connection is not yet open. The WebSocket is in the process of connecting.
OPEN (1): The connection is open, and communication is possible. You can send and receive data.
CLOSING (2): The connection is in the process of closing. This state is entered after the close() method is called.
CLOSED (3): The connection is closed or couldn't be opened. This is the final state, indicating that the connection is no longer available.
